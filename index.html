Places Must Visit

iberostar

the most recent time i've visited there is june 6th, 2023

The Iberostar Selection Paraíso Lindo is a family hotel on the Riviera Maya, located in the Paraíso Complex, along with 4 other 5-star hotels. If you want to enjoy the best food in a fun, ocean-themed setting decorated with recycled materials, visit our impressive Under the Sea restaurant. Don’t miss this unique dining opportunity! We’re here to take care of you and the planet, which is why we offer you our beachfront hotel on the age-old Caribbean, with a pool that mimics the ocean’s waves (but poses no danger to the little ones) and another pool designed as a Lazy River so you can drift along on a gentle current. And another 5 more pool areas so you can find your favorite! This family hotel not only has a Spa where you can unwind, it also has a fantastic water park with slides. Get ready to have fun with the whole family! Did you know that our Star Camp play area is made from more than 14,330 lb of recycled plastic? This is equivalent to 32,000 five-liter bottles and saves 65 trees’ worth of wood! And make sure you visit Tulum, the Mayan city. It’s less than an hour away from the hotel. Iberostar Selection Paraíso Lindo is a smoke-free hotel, so smoking is prohibited except in specifically designated areas.

xcaret

the most recent time i've visited there is june 9th, 2023

Xcaret is a water park, theme, amusement, eco-archaeological and more, in one place. Due to its privileged location in the jungle among underground rivers, cenotes and facing the Caribbean Sea, Xcaret has impressive scenarios where you can do different activities and enjoy them to the fullest. Besides that, Xcaret is a theme park in Cancun and Riviera Maya that celebrates Mexico’s present and rich past, a country known for its traditions, culture and folklore.



Movie Reviews

The Super Mario Bros Movie
Super excellent, for both adults and kids

The new Super Mario Bros movie is a delightful and nostalgic trip down memory lane for fans of the iconic video game franchise. With stunning animation that brings the Mushroom Kingdom to life in vibrant detail, the film successfully captures the essence of the beloved characters and their whimsical world. Chris Pratt as Mario, Charlie Day as Luigi, and the rest of the star-studded voice cast deliver memorable performances, infusing the characters with charm and humor. The movie's playful storyline, filled with classic Mario elements like power-ups, colorful enemies, and epic adventures, keeps both kids and adults engaged throughout. While some may miss the gaming experience, this cinematic adaptation is a fun-filled joyride that pays fitting tribute to the enduring legacy of Mario and his friends. It's a must-see for fans and a delightful family-friendly adventure for all.

Maleficent
Really Magical

"Maleficent" is a 2014 fantasy film that offers a fresh perspective on the classic fairy tale of "Sleeping Beauty." The movie explores the untold backstory of the iconic Disney villain, Maleficent, portrayed by Angelina Jolie. It delves into her transformation from a kind-hearted fairy living in the magical Moors to a vengeful sorceress after a betrayal. Maleficent places a curse on Princess Aurora, the king's daughter, which leads to the familiar tale of the sleeping beauty. However, as the story unfolds, it reveals themes of redemption, forgiveness, and the complexity of human nature. The film provides a unique twist on the traditional fairy tale, emphasizing the idea that "evil" characters may have more depth and humanity than initially perceived.

Spider-man: Into the Spider-verse
Awesome

Miles Morales catapults across the Multiverse, where he encounters a team of Spider-People charged with protecting its very existence. When the heroes clash on how to handle a new threat, Miles must redefine what it means to be a hero.
